Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

1. What Data We Access

When you log in with TikTok or Instagram, we access your public profile information, follower counts, and video performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares). We access only data belonging to the authenticated user.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to display analytics and performance insights to you within the app. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Performance metrics are stored locally to track trends over time. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond the platform APIs required to retrieve your information.

4. Third-Party Services

The app connects to TikTok and Instagram APIs to retrieve your data. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Deletion

You may revoke the app's access at any time through your TikTok or Instagram account settings. You may also request deletion of locally stored data by contacting the developer.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer via GitHub.
